About Me

I was the kid who had to know how everything worked. If it could come apart, you can rest assured that it would be taken apart. This childhood thirst for knowledge led to a ton of discovery...and to a lot of parental intervention. Until, to my parent's great relief, I discovered BASIC. This was magic to me, and the salvation of our household electronics. With a few line of code I could completely change the way a program functioned. And even better, I could CREATE!

Creation is the driving factor in my life, whether it's writing fiction, coding a functional application, or designing a beautiful website. The process of taking an ephemeral idea and turning it into something real is still magic. And it's how I choose to live my life.

I'm currently at the beginning of this adventure. I've chosen to code, to spend my life taking ideas and turning them into things. This is a challenging and exciting time...and at occasionally a bit frightening. But this is my choice. This is probably a good place to insert my favorite quote.

“If we listened to our intellect, we’d never have a love affair. We’d never have a friendship. Contact Form Project

If there is one thing a portfolio needs it's a way for potential clients or employers to get in touch with you, and the easier it is to make contact the better. That's what makes a contact form so important and that's why it is at the top of my project list. Because this page desperately needs one. This form and it's supporting serverside app will be designed to accept user input; validate the data; send it to a server; and from the server, email the collected data to the page owner.

Technologies to be used in this project include:

  • HTML to provide structure for the form
  • CSS for styling the form
  • Javascript for adding function to the form
  • jQuery to handle form submissions
  • PHP for server side processing of the data

Tic Tac Toe Project

I chose to make a simple Tic Tac Toe game for my next project for three reasons. First it will challenge me to build something other than web pages headless applications with what I have learned.
